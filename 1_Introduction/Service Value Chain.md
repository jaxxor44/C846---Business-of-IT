# Service Value Chain
- Transforms Demand into actual Value
- focused on mapping a process and identifying/eliminating waste.
- Universally applicable
- 6 key activities form multiple value streams (PIEDOD)
	- [[Plan]]
	- [[Improve]]
	- [[Engage]]
	- [[Design and transition]]
	- [[Obtain and build]]
	- [[Deliver and support]]
